History of Our Company

Established in 2016, ENDLOS is a diversified conglomerate headquartered in Ahmedabad, India, with an expansion in the United States through our sales office in Dallas, Texas. What started as a vision to create innovative, forward-thinking businesses has evolved into a dynamic group of companies:

Endlos Innovations, Endlos Technologies, and Endlos Hospitality. Each one is driven by a shared commitment to excellence and a focus on delivering exceptional value in its respective domain. At Endlos Hospitality, we apply our innovation-led approach to the hospitality furniture industry. Backed by a fully integrated manufacturing facility in India and deep expertise in hospitality design, we work closely with hotel owners across the United States to deliver high-quality, custom-made case goods. Every piece we create reflects our core belief in “The Endless Possibilities”. We believe in turning your customized ideas into outstanding results, from initial concept to final installation.
