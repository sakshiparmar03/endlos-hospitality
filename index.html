Project Management Overview

From initial layout planning to final installation, this process ensures efficient design, brand approval, quality production, and timely delivery, resulting in high-quality, customized spaces tailored to client needs.

1. Layout & Measurements

Clients provide site measurements, which we use to design a smart, space-efficient layout that balances functionality, aesthetics, and optimal use of available space.

2. 3D Visualization and Renders

We create high-quality 3D visualizations and renders to help clients clearly understand the final look and feel of their space.

3. Keyline Drawing

Detailed keyline drawings show exact dimensions and specifications, ensuring accurate and consistent production while helping to prevent errors and streamline the manufacturing process.

4. Brand Approval and Agreement Signing

Final designs are submitted for client approval to ensure satisfaction. Once approved, agreements are signed to officially begin production.

5. Production

Our manufacturing team begins production, maintaining strict quality control and closely following the approved designs and specifications to deliver flawless results on time.

6. Dispatch, Delivery and Installation

Completed products are carefully packed, dispatched to the project site, and installed efficiently to ensure a smooth handover.
